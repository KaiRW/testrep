Baked Ziti

Baked Ziti is a layered pasta dish similar to lasagna. It's filled with rich cheese, herbs, and marinara sauce. This Long Island staple is great for large gatherings. It's the perfect, comforting, crowd-pleasing dinner.

Ingredients

Yield: 8 servings

Total Cook Time: 65 minutes
  • 1 (16-ounce) box ziti noodles
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups homemade marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded skim mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  1. Heat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the ziti noodles according to package directions.
  3. Prepare the filling while the noodles cook: Mix together ricotta cheese, salt, pepper, dried basil, and dried oregano in a large bowl.
  4. Drain the noodles and mix the hot pasta with the ricotta.
  5. Coat an 8 x 8 baking dish with marinara sauce. Spoon a layer of the ziti mixture on top, then a layer of mozzarella. Continue layering the sauce, ziti, and mozzarella in order until the pan is full.
  6. Cover the top of the pasta with Â½ cup of mozzarella cheese and Parmesan.
  7. Spray a sheet of tin foil with oil and cover the baking dish. Bake the ziti for 30-35 minutes.
  8. Take the ziti out of the oven, remove the foil, and bake for another 10-15 minutes for a crunchy top. Serve and enjoy!
