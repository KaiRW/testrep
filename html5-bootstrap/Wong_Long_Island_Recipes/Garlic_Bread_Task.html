Garlic Bread

This classic garlic bread recipe is the perfect appetizer for any Italian-American dinner.

Ingredients

Yield: 8-10 servings

Total Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 baguette
  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cut the butter into small pieces and melt it in the microwave for ~45 seconds.
  3. Mix the garlic, salt, dried oregano, parsley, and thyme with the melted butter.
  4. Cut the baguette in half lengthwise.
  5. Spread the butter-herb mixture evenly onto the two slices.
  6. Tightly wrap the baguette slices in tin foil so they are fully covered. Bake for 15 minutes then unwrap the foil. Bake the slices again, uncovered, for another 5 minutes.
  7. Cut each slice into even, 1-inch pieces. Serve and enjoy!
